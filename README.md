**SUPER STORE SALES DASHBOARD**


**Project Objective**

Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard using a cleaned and structured sales dataset from Excel pivot analysis to visualize business performance and regional insights. Integrated Excel-based data modeling with Power BI to deliver a dynamic, data-driven view of customer and sales operations.

 **KPIS**

Total Sales – Overall revenue from all customer orders.

Total Profit – Earnings after subtracting costs.

Total Quantity Sold – Total number of items sold.

Sales by Region – Compares sales across different regions.

Sales by Category – Shows performance of each product category.

Sales by Segment – Tracks sales for each customer type.

Sales by Payment Mode – Displays preferred payment methods.

Sales by Ship Mode – Analyzes orders by delivery type.

Monthly Sales Trend – Shows sales growth over time.

Profit Trend – Tracks profit changes month by month.

Return Rate – Percentage of products returned by customers.

Shipping Delay – Average days between order and delivery.

**Process**

1.Cleaned and organized the sales data in Excel.

2.Created pivot tables to analyze sales, profit, and returns.

3.Imported the cleaned data into Power BI.

4.Designed interactive visuals for regional and category insights.

5.Built and published a dynamic dashboard showing overall performance.

**Dash Board**


**Final Conclusion**

This project provides a clear view of sales and operational performance using Excel and Power BI.It highlights key insights on profit, customer behavior, and delivery efficiency across regions and categories.The dashboard helps monitor business growth and supports data-driven decision-making.
